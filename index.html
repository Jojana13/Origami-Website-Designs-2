Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they ise to wrap around tree branches

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  1. Bamboo is critical to their diet
  2. Pandas eat consistently for 12-14 hours a day
  3. A panda's paw has six digits—five fingers and an opposable pseudo-thumb
